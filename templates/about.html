Welcome to R.E.F.C !

Recommendation Engine for Cricket or R.E.F.C is a web platform that would help in selecting best players for our team. Recommendation Engines are applications or softwares that assess the existing past data and provide a recommendation or suggestion for the future choices. This recommendation engine will look at the data of past performances of players and will recommend suitable players based on the match conditions of the upcoming match. The match conditions could include type of pitch and weather conditions. This platform will provide the selectors with a probable team and will help them to make the team selection process more efficient, easy and quick. We are updating the engine efficiency continuously and are trying to provide the authorities more insight in the selection process.
